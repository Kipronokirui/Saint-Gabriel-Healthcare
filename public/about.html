About Us

The concept of Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services' foundation was inspired by, Mr. Gabriel Kwame Greene(pictured), who experienced extensive health care services for 7 years, prior to his death, after a hit-and-run accident, which remains an unsolved case.

Saint Gabriel Home Health Services is a residential home health agency dedicated to providing in-home care, skilled nursing and personal care for both adult and the pediatric population at the comfort of their own homes. We provide high quality care, pursuant to the six domains of the Institute of Medicine (IOM), in terms of equity, safety, efficiency, effectiveness, timelines, and patient centeredness, including compliance with applicable State and Federal rules and regulations.

We strive to maintain an integrated and coordinated in-home care service utilizing multidisciplinary approach. In all that we do, our clients and their families are our priority.

About Our Facility

Mission Statement

Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services is a residential service agency incorporated to provide in-home care, skilled nursing, and personal care for both adult and the pediatric population in the comfort of their own homes. We are committed to using the latest nursing practices in assessing, planning, and implementing, evaluating, and taking care of our clients. We believe that life is precious and must be maintained and sustained at all ages, especially at old age. Those with mental and developmental disabilities should be treated with care, love, and dignity.


Skilled & Non-Skilled Service Policy

Saint Gabriel Health Care Service will provide skilled and non-skilled services to pediatric and adult patients in Baltimore City, Anne Arundel, Baltimore and Howard, Prince George`s counties in the State of Maryland.

Our Goals

  • To maintain an integrated and coordinated in-home care service utilizing multidisciplinary approach.
  • To achieve flexibility in providing care, follow-ups, and good communication.
  • To protect the health and safety of our clients/patients and ensure high levels of confidence in our system through good policies and procedures.

Objective(s)

  • To deinstitutionalize care-giving. Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services beleives that the best place to care for our population is in their individual homes and community.
  • To ensure reliable scheduling of staff duties and timely delivery of services.
  • Follow all licensure, state, federal and local government laws and regulations.
  • Ensure availability of a 24/7 service to all our clients.
