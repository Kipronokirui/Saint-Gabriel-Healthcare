What We Offer

Skilled & Non-Skilled Service Policy

Saint Gabriel Health Care Service will provide skilled and non-skilled services to pediatric and adult patients in Baltimore City, Anne Arundel, Baltimore and Howard, Prince George`s counties in the State of Maryland.


Saint Gabriel Health Care Services Offers:

  • 24/7 nursing care and telephone access.
  • Supportive, efficient, and administrative staff.
  • Screened well trained and experienced personal care providers.
  • Free initial consultation.
  • Services provided by Registered Nurses(RNs), Licensed Practical Nurses(LPNs), Certified Nursing Aides(CNAs), Certified Medication Technicians(CMTs), and Home Health Aide(HHA)

The skilled nursing services provided includes:

  • Wound care.
  • Disease Management.
  • Ostomy care.
  • Blood pressure Management.
  • Nasogastric/Gastrostomy/Jejunostomy Tube care.
  • Tracheostomy and ventilator care.

The non-skilled nursing services provided includes:

  • Assisting with Activities of Daily Living(ADLs), including but not limited to dressing, eating, bathing, and other personal care needs.
